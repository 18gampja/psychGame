About this Project:
Couch Counseling is a video-training software made by several Penn State Abington Computer Science Students for Professor Lavetsky and the Rehabilitation and Human Services committee at Penn State to help prepare psychology students for at-home counseling sessions with members of their community. This project utilizes professor-curated videos to immerse the student in the case they are working on, allowing them to simulate traveling to a client's home, entering their house, and administering an at-home counseling session, all from the comfort of their computer.
